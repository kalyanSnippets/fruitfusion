Fruit Fusion

Fruit Fusion is fully registered and licensed from NSW food authority.

We are the vending company that gives the customers organic fruit salads overall Sydney Australia depending on the distance it takes maximum 45minutes to deliver. Our basic idea is to provide customise fruit items according to customers choice via using our own app named “fruit fusion” where customers can select all kind of fruits whether seasonal or non-seasonal which will help them to carry on their work or can even select by just sitting at their places.

We are quite aware that in order to become number one choice of customers to select their favourite fruit items, we are promising to provide fresh and healthy fruits. We are open to use the latest fast technological devices which will accept the orders fast and deliver them on time.

Our Business: Fruit Fusion will ensure that we will go all the way to make available wide range of fresh cut fruits salad in Sydney. Our product offerings are listed below: Fresh fruits such as Banana, Pineapple, papaya, Strawberry, Blueberry, Raspberry, Plum, Mango, Apple, Cucumber, Dragon fruit, Oranges, Grapes, Avocado and a host of other fruits. Our products will be made of local fruits, free of additives and artificial preservatives. There will be no need for adding sugar as the fruit contain natural sugars.
